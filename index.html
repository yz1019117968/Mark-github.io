Short Bio

I'm currently a third-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at City University of Hong Kong. I'm very fortunate to work under the supervision of Dr. Jacky Keung during my Ph.D. study. From Jan. to Sept. 2022, I was very honored to be a visiting student in the Key Laboratory of High Confidence Software Technologies, Ministry of Education (Peking University), where my supervisors there are Prof. Zhi Jin and Dr. Ge Li. My current research focuses on software engineering, natural language processing, program comprehension, and blockchain. Before pursuing the Ph.D., I received my B.A. degree in Information System from the University of Jinan (UJN) in 2018 and received my M.S. degree in Computer Science from the CityU in 2019.
