Short Bio

I'm currently a tenure-track Assistant Professor in the School of Computer Science and Technology at Shandong University (SDU). In Oct. 2023, I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from the City University of Hong Kong (CityU), under the supervision of Prof. Jacky Wai Keung. From Jan. to Sept. 2022, I was very honored to visit the Key Laboratory of High Confidence Software Technologies, Ministry of Education (Peking University), where my supervisors are Prof. Zhi Jin and Prof. Ge Li. My current research focuses on software engineering and natural language processing. Before pursuing the Ph.D., I received my B.Mgt. degree in Information System from the University of Jinan (UJN) in 2018 and received my M.Sc. degree in Computer Science with Distinction from the CityU in 2019.
